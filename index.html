You like cycling or running? You can support Ukraine heroically fighting until the end in a biggest war in Europe since WW2 by doing what you love. Here you will find cycling and running routes for different cities in the world shaped as a map of Ukraine.
The first cycling route in a geographical shape of a Ukraine was made by Taras Gulevaty from Kiev. He created two routes in Kyiv, one 130 km long, another for 39 km.

The 130 km route in Kyiv was first conquered on 2018.
In July 2019 the same route was accomplished without a bike! People finished 132.8 km run in 14 hours and 8 minutes.
